## THE NICHE

### Italian Freelancers Who Deduct Expenses from Receipts

**Profile:**
- Partita IVA forfettaria or ordinaria
- 5-20 clients
- Receives 20-100 paper/digital receipts per month (suppliers, services, travel)
- Currently: shoebox of receipts + Excel + manual invoice creation
- Pain: Hours of data entry. Lost receipts. Lost deductions.

**Why this niche?**
1. **OCR is the wedge** — Receipt → Invoice in 10 seconds is a magical moment
2. **Italian tax logic is the moat** — VAT 22%, withholding tax, ritenuta d'acconto
3. **Competitors don't serve this** — Expensify does receipts but not Italian invoicing. Invoice Ninja does invoicing but not receipt OCR.
4. **€5 is impulse purchase** — No procurement cycle, no approval needed

## DEFENSIBILITY

### Why Competitors Can't Easily Copy

1. **Italian tax logic** — ritenuta d'acconto, withholding, VAT 22%, split payment. Requires Italian developer.
2. **OCR training data** — Your regex patterns are trained on Italian receipts (P.IVA format, date format, amount format). Expensify's OCR is English-first.
3. **Community trust** — Being "the Italian freelancer tool" builds community moat.
4. **Simplicity** — Big tools can't get simpler (their customers demand complexity). You start simple and stay focused.
