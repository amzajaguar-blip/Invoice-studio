## Why Would Someone Buy ONLY for OCR?

### Target: Italian Freelancers Who Receive Paper Invoices

**Pain**: 
- Client gives them a paper receipt for a €2,000 expense
- They must manually create an invoice from it for tax purposes
- Or they lose the deduction
- Takes 5-10 minutes per receipt
- They have 20-50 receipts/month = 2-8 hours of manual work

**Current Solutions**:
- Do it manually (slow, error-prone)
- Use Expensify (€15/month, English-only, no Italian tax logic)
- Use Dext (€30/month, overkill)
- Ignore it (lose tax deductions)

**InvoiceStudio OCR offers**:
- Point camera at receipt
- OCR extracts: vendor, P.IVA, amount, date
- One-click creates invoice with Italian VAT logic
- PDF generated
- Done in 10 seconds

**Value**: 2-8 hours/month saved
**Willingness to pay**: €5-€10/month without thinking

## Risk: What If OCR Accuracy Is Bad?

**Mitigation**:
- Review step is mandatory (user corrects before save)
- Show confidence scores prominently
- "Low confidence" warning when overall < 60%
- Allow manual creation as fallback (already exists)
- Position as "OCR-assisted" not "OCR-only"
