## STRATEGIC INSIGHT

The value map reveals a clear pattern:

**High-ROI features are clustered around the OCR → Invoice → Paid flow.**

**Low-ROI features are scattered across expansion areas (AI, team, white-label).**

The product strategy should be:

1. **Perfect the core flow** (OCR → Invoice → Paid) — 80% of value
2. **Add legal compliance** (Fattura Elettronica) — unlocks market
3. **Add stickiness** (recurring invoices, reminders) — prevents churn
4. **Ignore everything else** until the core flow converts users at >5%

**Do not build another feature until a user can go from photo to paid invoice in under 60 seconds.**
